Our Story

Established in 2004, 'MAULI Krushi Seva Kendra' has been serving farmers with dedication and commitment.
Our founder, Mr. Pramod Shirfule , started this venture with a vision to empower farmers by providing them with the best agricultural supplies and expert advice.
Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the agricultural sector, known for our quality products and customer service.
We have a well-stocked shop with a wide range of agricultural supplies, and our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you. We are committed to providing you with the best quality products at the cheapest prices. Our shop is equipped with the latest technology to ensure that you get the best products available in the market. We are dedicated to helping farmers achieve their goals and improve their livelihoods. We are proud to be a trusted name in the agricultural sector, known for our quality products and exceptional customer service. We look forward to serving you and contributing to the growth of the agricultural community.
We have received several awards and recognitions for our commitment to quality and service.
Our dedication to the agricultural community has been acknowledged by various organizations, and we continue to strive for excellence in everything we do.
